What is StrayConnected?

StrayConnected is a mobile application created to bring together compassionate people and stray animals who are searching for a safe, permanent home. The app connects adopters with trusted shelters and volunteers, transforming the adoption process into a simple, transparent, and meaningful experience.

With StrayConnected, you can explore a wide range of animal profiles, each carefully presented with photos, age, breed, personality traits, and verified health information. This allows you to truly understand an animal's story and needs before making a decision.
